Tidsskriftet

  • Interaction design
  • Prototyping
  • iOS development

Tidskriftet is an iPad app made for the Norwegian Medical Association. Tidsskriftet is their biweekly medical publication, and I was involved in the process of designing and programming a magazine concept for the iPad. Using the app, doctors can search for published articles as far as back to 2008, download issues for offline usage, and create bookmarks and highlight text for easy retrieval of interesting articles.

Download Tidsskriftet in the App Store.

Project with Nikolai Strandskogen at Netlife Research.
