Hva koster tannlegen

  • Concept
  • Interaction design
  • Prototyping

Hva koster tannlegen is a price portal for Norwegian dentists. Before this web page was launched, it was hard for cosumers to get an overview of dentist prices in their area. The portal is also increasing the competiton between dentists, driving the prices down for consumers. I helped creating the concept, interaction design and interactive prototype.

Project for Netlife Research with William Hindson.
